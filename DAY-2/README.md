# Northwind Database Analysis

## Database Overview

The Northwind database is a sample relational database representing a trading company. It contains information about customers, orders, employees, products, suppliers, shippers, and categories.

---

# Business Questions

1. Which are the top 10 selling products?
2. Who are the top 10 customers by revenue?
3. What are the monthly sales trends?
4. Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
5. Which customers purchase most frequently?

---

# Tools Used

- SQLite
- SQL
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

---

# Exploratory Analysis

The SQL query results were imported into Pandas for analysis. Descriptive statistics and visualizations were created to better understand sales performance and customer behavior.

---

# Business Insights

### Insight 1
A small number of products contribute a significant portion of total sales.

### Insight 2
The highest-revenue customers are valuable and should be targeted with loyalty programs.

### Insight 3
Monthly sales show seasonal fluctuations, indicating periods of high demand.

### Insight 4
Certain product categories consistently outperform others in terms of revenue.

### Insight 5
Customers with high purchase frequency represent strong long-term business relationships.
